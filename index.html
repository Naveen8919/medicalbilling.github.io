1. What is medical billing?

Medical billing is the process of submitting claims to insurance companies and patients to receive payment for healthcare services. It involves translating medical diagnoses and procedures into standardized codes, creating and submitting claims, and following up on payments and resolving any issues to ensure the healthcare provider is paid. This is a critical component of a healthcare facility's revenue cycle management.

2.What is Revenue Cycle Managment(RCM)? In US healthcare, RCM stands for Revenue Cycle Management, the entire administrative and financial process healthcare providers use to ensure they are paid for the services they provide to patients. It connects clinical functions (like patient care and documentation) with business operations (like billing and collections).
